Tools

Fotografix provides the following tools for working with images:

ToolFunctionShortcut
Rectangular MarqueeSelects a rectangular regionS
Elliptical MarqueeSelects an elliptical regionE
Magic WandSelects regions of similar colourW
MoveMoves a layer or selectionV
TransformResizes, flips or rotates a layerR
RulerMeasures the distance/angle between two pointsL
BrushPaints with a brushB
EraserErases portions of the imageZ
CloneClones a portion of an image at another locationC
FillFills regions of similar colourF
GradientDraws a gradientG
TextAdds text to the imageT
DropperPicks a colour from the imageY
HandPans through the imageH
ZoomZooms in to or out of the imageJ
