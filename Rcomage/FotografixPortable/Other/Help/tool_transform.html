Using the Transform tool

The Transform tool is used to transform a layer in various ways, such as resizing, flipping or rotating it.

  1. Choose the layer that you would like to transform.
  2. Select the Transform tool by clicking on its icon in the tool box or by pressing R.
  3. A box with resize handles appears around the layer.
    • To resize the contents of the layer, click and drag the handles around the box.
    • To flip or rotate the layer, right-click inside the box and select one of the options.
    • To move the layer, click inside the box and drag. For more control over moving a layer, use the Move tool.
